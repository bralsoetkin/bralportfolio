I’m Soetkin, born in Belgium in 1995, and I’m studying illustration at Luca School of Arts in Ghent.
Previously I spent a semester at HEAR in Strasbourg.
Illustration for me is not only about making books, but also to express oneself in autonomous images, three-dimensional works or even in theatre. The main purpose for me is to tell a story.

My work is based on situations and people I observe on the street, in trains, etc... Especially the extraordinary or bizarre scenes got a place in my sketchbook. Later I select and combine things in a painting or drawing. They become dreamlike images in which fiction and reality meet and become balanced. This balance on the edge of recognition and alienation always plays a key role in my work. By taking a situation and putting it in a new context, I invite people to look different on everyday life. Moreover, my work is an invitation to laugh together about humanity.
